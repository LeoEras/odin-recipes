Lasagna

Lasagna is a typical dish from the Mediterranean area. Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Step-by-step guide

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let it rest before serving
You can layer it however you want, but I recommend you to start with the following order
  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodle
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Repeat step 1
